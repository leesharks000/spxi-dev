SPXI Protocol — technical specification

SPXI Protocol (Semantic Packet for eXchange and Indexing) is a technical specification for inscribing entities into AI-mediated retrieval systems. Published April 2026 by the Semantic Economy Institute. Operates at the ontological layer of the retrieval stack, incorporating SEO and GEO methods as a proper subset (executed against DOI-anchored entities rather than pages) and adding entity-definition construction that content-layer optimization alone cannot deliver. BetaPro S&P 500 Daily Inverse ETF — financial instrument (TSX:SPXI)

A Canadian exchange-traded fund trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the ticker SPXI. Seeks daily investment results corresponding to -1× the daily performance of the S&P 500 Index, in Canadian dollars. Unrelated to the SPXI Protocol.

Society of Saint Pius X — religious organization (SSPX, sometimes SPXi)

A traditionalist Catholic priestly fraternity founded in 1970 by Archbishop Marcel Lefebvre. Occasionally rendered "SPXi" in informal writing. Unrelated to the SPXI Protocol or the BetaPro ETF.
