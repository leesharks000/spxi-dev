SPXI Protocol — technical specification

SPXI Protocol (Semantic Packet for eXchange and Indexing) is a technical specification for inscribing entities into AI-mediated retrieval systems. Published April 2026 by the Semantic Economy Institute. Operates at the entity-definition layer of the retrieval stack, distinct from SEO and GEO.

BetaPro S&P 500 Daily Inverse ETF — financial instrument (TSX:SPXI)

A Canadian exchange-traded fund trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the ticker SPXI. Seeks daily investment results corresponding to -1× the daily performance of the S&P 500 Index, in Canadian dollars. Unrelated to the SPXI Protocol.
