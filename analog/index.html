SPXI · Analog

Ink-on-paper inscription as machine-eligible scholarly input.

The SPXI Analog line extends SPXI to non-digital inputs. Where the core SPXI protocol operates on digital entities inscribed with structured markup, SPXI Analog specifies how handwritten scholarly artifacts — marginalia, letters, notebooks, ink-and-paper drafts — enter the same protocol's semantic layer through a disciplined machine-facing companion layer.
